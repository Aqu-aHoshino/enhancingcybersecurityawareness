Welcome to the Cybersecurity Awareness Research Website

This website was developed as the official research output of ICT 12 – St. Veronica students from Lian Institute Inc. The project focuses on the growing importance of cybersecurity awareness among senior high school students who regularly interact with digital technologies. As schools increasingly rely on online platforms for communication, research, and academic collaboration, students are exposed to various cybersecurity threats that may compromise their personal information and digital safety. Cybersecurity refers to the protection of digital systems, networks, and information from cyber threats such as hacking, malware, and unauthorized access (Giannakas et al., 2019). Through this website, readers can explore detailed explanations of cybersecurity threats, practical strategies to avoid them, and research findings collected from senior high school students regarding their knowledge, attitudes, and behaviors related to online safety.

The purpose of this website is not only to present academic research findings but also to provide a reliable educational resource for students, teachers, and parents who wish to improve their understanding of cybersecurity practices. The website includes comprehensive explanations of common digital threats such as phishing, malware, identity theft, cyberbullying, ransomware, and other forms of cybercrime that students may encounter online. Each section provides examples, prevention strategies, and references to research literature that explain why cybersecurity awareness is essential in modern education. In addition to these educational resources, the website also features CyVoid, an interactive chatbot that allows users to ask questions about cybersecurity threats and safe digital practices.

By presenting both theoretical knowledge and real research findings, the website aims to encourage responsible digital behavior among students and promote safer participation in online environments. The research presented here highlights the importance of combining cybersecurity awareness with practical skills that allow students to recognize threats, respond to cyber incidents, and maintain safe online habits. Ultimately, strengthening cybersecurity awareness among students can reduce the risks associated with cybercrime while supporting a safer and more responsible digital learning environment.
