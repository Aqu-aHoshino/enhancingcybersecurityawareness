Purpose of the Website

This website was created as the official research output of ICT 12 – St. Veronica students from Lian Institute Inc. The project focuses on understanding cybersecurity awareness among senior high school students and presenting reliable information about the digital threats that learners may encounter when using online technologies. As digital tools become essential for communication, research, and academic activities, students are increasingly exposed to cyber risks that may compromise their personal data, privacy, and emotional well-being. The purpose of this website is to present research-based knowledge about cybersecurity threats and demonstrate practical ways that students can protect themselves when interacting with digital platforms. By combining theoretical literature, research findings, and real-world examples, the website aims to provide a clear and educational guide for students, teachers, and parents who want to promote safer digital practices.

Another important goal of this research website is to promote cybersecurity awareness among young internet users. Studies show that students who understand online risks and digital security practices are more likely to behave responsibly in digital environments and avoid sharing sensitive personal information (Kovacevic, Putnik, & Toskovic, 2020). In the Philippines, students actively use social media platforms, online learning systems, and communication applications, which increases their exposure to cyber threats such as phishing, identity theft, and cyberbullying (Reyes, 2019). Without proper awareness and guidance, these threats may lead to financial risks, emotional stress, or academic difficulties. This website therefore serves as both a research presentation and a cybersecurity education resource that encourages responsible digital behavior among students.

In addition to explaining cybersecurity threats, the website also presents the findings of a study conducted among senior high school students at Lian Institute Inc. The research examined students’ familiarity with cybersecurity software, their level of awareness regarding online threats, and their experiences with digital security risks. By analyzing the results of this study, the researchers were able to identify gaps in students’ cybersecurity knowledge and recommend strategies for improving digital safety education in schools. The information presented throughout this website aims to help readers better understand why cybersecurity awareness is essential in modern education and how individuals can contribute to a safer digital environment.
